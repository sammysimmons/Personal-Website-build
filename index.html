Vitality Website

With active being the fortay of my expertise, from working in the health & fitness industry. The active page for Vitality I took alot of my inspiration from Apples user interface, by including video motions elements, which were also suppose to work well with the ingredients toggle, however as you have to comply with certain rules for stating medical facts, without backed research I had to loose the toggle element from my design.

I did however succeed in conveying, the message of CBD can be consumed anywhere, its not just for fitness its a lifestyle, by using complimentary imagery for the videos. The parallax elements, on the website also compliment the video elements.
